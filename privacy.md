AegisPub is a static publication. We do not collect personal data.

## What We Do Not Collect

- No cookies are set
- No analytics are loaded
- No tracking pixels or third-party scripts are embedded in this site
- No user accounts, no registration, no login

## Self-Hosted Assets

All fonts and scripts served by this site are self-hosted. No requests are made to Google Fonts, CDNs, or other external services that could log your IP address as a byproduct of serving assets.

## RSS

If you subscribe via RSS, your feed reader fetches `/feed.xml` from our server. This is a standard HTTP request. We do not log or store these requests beyond what GitHub Pages infrastructure may retain in its standard server logs, over which we have no control.

## Third-Party Links

This site links to external platforms including Medium, Reddit, Facebook, X/Twitter, and Instagram. Those platforms operate under their own privacy policies. We do not control them.
