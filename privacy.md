**Personal Data** means any information that relates to an identified or identifiable individual. This includes but is not limited to your name, email address, location, IP address, payment details, and any other data that can directly or indirectly identify you.

**Non-Personal Data** means information that cannot be used to directly identify an individual, such as browser type, general location, device information, website usage statistics, and other anonymised or aggregated data.

**Cookies** are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us track your activity and preferences on our Platforms and improve the user experience.

**AI-Generated Content** refers to content on AegisPub's Platforms created with the help of Artificial Intelligence tools. While AI helps create content, human oversight is applied where necessary to ensure accuracy and quality.

**Service Providers** are third-party companies or individuals contracted to perform certain functions or services on behalf of AegisPub, such as web hosting, data analysis, or customer service.

**Analytics Tools** are software or services used to track and analyse user behaviour and interactions on our Platforms, helping us improve content, performance, and user experience.

**GDPR** refers to the General Data Protection Regulation, a legal framework that sets guidelines for the collection and processing of personal information from individuals in the European Union.

**CCPA** refers to the California Consumer Privacy Act, which provides California residents with certain rights regarding their personal information.

## 1. Information We Collect

When you interact with AegisPub's Platforms, we may collect both personal and non-personal information.

### Personal Information

Personal information is data that can be used to identify you directly or indirectly, such as:

- Name

We collect personal information only when you voluntarily provide it to us, such as when you sign up for newsletters, comment on posts, or contact us.
