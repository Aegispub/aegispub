Definitions

AegisPub refers to the website aegispub.com, its social media accounts, digital platforms, and any services or content associated with it.

Platforms refers to the digital properties owned or managed by AegisPub, including but not limited to:

  • aegispub.com
  • Facebook

Personal Data means any information that relates to an identified or identifiable individual. This includes but is not limited to your name, email address, location, IP address, payment details, and any other data that can directly or indirectly identify you.

Non-Personal Data means information that cannot be used to directly identify an individual, such as browser type, general location, device information, website usage statistics, and other anonymised or aggregated data.

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us track your activity and preferences on our Platforms and improve the user experience.

AI-Generated Content refers to content on AegisPub’s Platforms created with the help of Artificial Intelligence tools. While AI helps create content, human oversight is applied where necessary to ensure accuracy and quality.

Analytics Tools are software or services used to track and analyse user behaviour and interactions on our Platforms, helping us improve content, performance, and user experience.

GDPR refers to the General Data Protection Regulation, a legal framework that sets guidelines for the collection and processing of personal information from individuals in the European Union.

CCPA refers to the California Consumer Privacy Act, which provides California residents with certain rights regarding their personal information.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with AegisPub’s Platforms, we may collect both personal and non-personal information.

Personal Information

Personal information is data that can be used to identify you directly or indirectly, such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Social media handles
  • IP address
  • Payment information (if applicable)

We collect personal information only when you voluntarily provide it to us, such as when you sign up for newsletters, comment on posts, or contact us.

Non-Personal Information

Non-personal information includes data that cannot be used to directly identify you:

  • Browser type
  • Device information
  • Website usage statistics
  • Geographical data (city-level location)
  • Cookies and tracking data

2. How We Collect Information

Directly from You

When you interact with AegisPub’s Platforms, such as subscribing to a newsletter or filling out a form, we collect the personal information you provide.

Automatically Through Cookies and Analytics

We use cookies and other tracking technologies to automatically collect information about how you interact with our Platforms. Our Platforms also use analytics tools to collect data on website traffic, visitor behaviour, and usage patterns.

You can adjust your cookie preferences through your browser settings. However, some features of our Platforms may not function properly without cookies.

From Third Parties

We may collect information from third parties, such as social media platforms and analytics providers. This data may be combined with information you provide to enhance your experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • Processing transactions and responding to enquiries
  • Sending newsletters, updates, and promotional materials
  • Analysing user behaviour and improving website performance
  • Monitoring for potential security risks or misuse of our Platforms
  • Complying with legal requirements including GDPR and CCPA

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your data in the following scenarios:

Some content on AegisPub’s Platforms may be generated or enhanced using Artificial Intelligence tools. We strive to ensure that AI-generated content aligns with user privacy and ethical standards.
